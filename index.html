The wage gap is a significant issue companies have to deal with. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the wage gap is the difference between the median wage and the highest wage in a given industry.

This report portrays the company's demographics and the average salaries per demographic to assess whether the wage gap is prevalent in our company.
